Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 28, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Lightway ("the App") handles user information during its use.

1. Information Collection and Use

Lightway allows users to voluntarily enter information such as:

  • Favorite books, movies, subjects, and hobbies
  • Country or region
  • Languages spoken
  • Academic grades or performance information

This information is used solely to generate personalized recommendations for suitable university majors and institutions.

2. Data Storage

All user-entered data is stored locally on the user’s device. The App does not permanently store personal data on external servers or developer-controlled databases.

3. Use of OpenAI API

To generate recommendations, Lightway sends relevant user-provided information to the OpenAI API for processing.

  • Data is transmitted securely
  • Data is used only to generate responses
  • The developer does not retain copies of this data on external servers

OpenAI’s handling of data is governed by their own Privacy Policy: https://openai.com/policies/privacy

4. Data Sharing

Lightway does not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties, except as required to provide its core functionality through the OpenAI API.

5. Log Data

In case of an application error, the App may collect anonymous technical data such as crash logs to improve performance and stability. This data does not personally identify users.

6. Data Security

Reasonable security measures are used to protect data during transmission. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

7. User Control

Users can modify or delete their information at any time within the App. Uninstalling the App will remove all locally stored data.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
