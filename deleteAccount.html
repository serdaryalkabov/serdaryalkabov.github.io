Account Deletion – MindDuel

If you would like to delete your MindDuel account and remove your associated data, you can follow the instructions below.

How to Request Account Deletion

You can request account deletion by contacting us directly at:

Email: yalkabovserdar07@gmail.com

Please include the email address associated with your MindDuel account so we can verify ownership.

What Happens When Your Account Is Deleted?

When you request deletion, we will:

  • Delete your account information (email, password)
  • Delete usage-related data associated with your account
  • Remove any stored content linked to your account

Voice input and debate data processed by the app are temporary and not stored long-term.

How Long Does Deletion Take?

Account deletion requests will be processed within 7 days of receiving your email.
